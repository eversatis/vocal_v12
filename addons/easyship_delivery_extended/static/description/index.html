Easyship Odoo Integration

Streamline your shipping processes and reduce shipment preparation time by integrating Easyship with Odoo. Easyship allows you to connect with 250+ shipping solutions in one simple integration. Automatically submit order information from Odoo to Easyship and get Shipping label, Order Tracking number and Shipping Cost from Easyship to Odoo. All available service and rate visible in sale order, select any one and add to order.
